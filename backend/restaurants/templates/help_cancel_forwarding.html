How to remove call forwarding from your phone

If your Concierge AI service is paused, you can stop forwarding calls so customers aren't left on hold. Find your carrier below, dial the code from your business phone, then press Call.

Quick fix for most phones: dial ##21# and press Call. This cancels unconditional call forwarding on most GSM carriers worldwide.

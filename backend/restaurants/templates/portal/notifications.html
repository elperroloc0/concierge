Install Concierge first

On iPhone, push notifications work only when Concierge is installed as an app:

  1. Tap Share at the bottom of Safari
  2. Scroll down → Add to Home Screen
  3. Open Concierge from the home-screen icon
  4. Come back here and tap Enable on this device

Requires iOS 16.4 or later.
